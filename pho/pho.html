Design Tips

    Understand the Space:
  • Before starting any design project, take the time to understand the space you are working with. Measure the dimensions, note any architectural features, and consider how the space will be used.
    • Create a Focal Point:
  • Every room should have a focal point that draws the eye. This could be a piece of art, a piece of furniture, or even a unique architectural feature. Arrange your furniture and decor around this focal point.

    Balance Function and Aesthetics:
  • While it's important for a space to look good, it should also be functional. Choose furniture and decor that serve a purpose and enhance the usability of the room.

    • Use a Cohesive Color Scheme:
  • Select a color palette that reflects the mood you want to create. Stick to a cohesive color scheme throughout the room to create a harmonious look. Use accent colors to add interest and contrast.
